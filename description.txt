A Mod dedicated to the 187 Strassenbande.


DISCLAIMER: This mod is inteded to be on the more overpowered side.

Included are Weapons, Equipables and a Potion.

The only Weapon currently available is the CL500, it shoots Gzuz (Which is a german Rapper). 

The two Equipables are the "Köftespiess" and "187StrassenbandeLogo".
These are very overpowered, therefore be used with more Hardcore-Mods.

The Potion ingame is the "Ayran".
It grants the "Frische Ayran" Buff, which gives you incredible Speed and more.
